ABOUT US

Guided by a strong set of values, Russell set out to build a "different" kind of modern company, one founded on compassion and ethics, aiming to influence society in both fashion and social responsibility. Simply put, Mr. Graf created a brand with a conscience; a brand that cares.

First and foremost, we are committed to our people; those who have helped us get to where we are today. Though our workforce is as diverse as our geographical imprint, a common thread remains – our team members are passionate, productive and motivated. We provide our team with an abundance of opportunities to help them realize their full potential, because at our core, we believe that when one person grows, so too does the company. The result is a productive and fast-paced culture where performance standards are high and a healthy work ethic and overall excellence is celebrated.

BBold is also well known for its commitment to society. We place a premium on being a good corporate citizen by working to enrich the communities in which we live and work. Over the years, we have supported many vital philanthropic causes such as War Child, YouthAIDS, CANFAR, Youth Fusion and the Cure Foundation – and that’s just to name a few.

FAQ'S

How do I place an order?

+ Expand

Ordering online is easy! When you find an item you would like to purchase, select a color and size and click "add to bag" button. At this point you may continue to shop for more items, add the ones you'd like to purchase to your bag.When you finish shopping, click “shopping bag" button at the top-right corner of the page to check out. You will find a quick summary of your order on the right side of the following page. Follow the check out process step by step.If all looks well, click "place order". Then, your order is successfully placed.If you'd like to modify your order, you may do so by removing or changing the quantity of your order.Once you are done, click “proceed to checkout” or “Check out with PayPal.

How do I check the status of my order?

+ Expand

To check the status of your order after it has been placed, simply:1. Click "my account" at the top of any page2. Unless you are already signed in, sign in to your account using the email address and password you used when you made your purchase3. On the left side of the page, click “view all” under "My Orders"4. The status of your order should appear on the page. For detailed order information, click “view order”.5. If your order is listed as shipped, you can track your shipment under "track your orders" on the bottom of the page.

How do I track my package?

+ Expand

You can easily track your package by signing into your account using the email address and password you used when you made your purchase, and clicking "track your orders" on My Orders-View Order page.
